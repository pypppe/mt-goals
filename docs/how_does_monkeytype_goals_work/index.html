How does Monkeytype Goals work?

MonkeyType Goals works in a simple way. Your computer!

MonkeyType Goals is designed to not be hosted on a server, so we use your computer, aka LocalStorage to store your goals in Monkeytype Goals.

We designed it this way so there is no database that can be hacked. However, if you do change your computers it is important to remember what goals you had so you can Pin them onto your new device.
